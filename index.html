Obsidian

A second brain,
for you, forever.

Obsidian is a powerful knowledge base that works on top of a local folder of plain text Markdown files.

“a second brain”

The human brain is non-linear: we jump from idea to idea, all the time. Your second brain should work the same.

In Obsidian, making and following [[connections]] is frictionless. Tend to your notes like a gardener; at the end of the day, sit back and marvel at your own knowledge graph.

“for you”

TNote-taking is incredibly personal. Tried every app, but there's always something not quite right? You deserve better.

Obsidian is built to be extensible. With 22 core plugins and 129 community plugins, set up your own toolkit and get running in minutes.

You'll even be able to install third party plugins or build your own once Obsidian reaches v1.0. Sky's the limit.

  • Graph view

    View a graphical representation of all your notes and connections, as well as open a local graph for your individual note.

  • Tag pane

    All your tags and nested tags in one place for quick access any information.

  • Daily notes

    Create today's note with custom date format and optional use a template.

  • Quick switcher

    Find any note in a moment when you think it. Jump to or create note with keyboard only.

  • Outline

    Shows the list of headings for the current note, and allows you to navigate to another section by clicking on a heading.

  • Templates

    Create daily notes, week notes, month plan, summary templates. Use predefined snippets of text into your current note.
